You will be given a random Target Score at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount to your Current Score.

You win the round by matching your Current Score to the Target Score. If your Current Score goes over the Target Score you lose the round.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each round when the game starts the Target Score and the value of the crystals will reset.
